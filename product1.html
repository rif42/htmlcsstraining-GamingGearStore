Product Description


Ducky One TKL is a fully mechanical keyboard aimed for computer enthusiasts. Ducky One uses CherryMX switch, which retains the best quality and longevity in keyboard switch market. The TKL (tenkeyless) size is just right for most people out there; compact enough to carry around anywhere, while maintaining all the vital functions you'd want from a keyboard. The muted white color is perfect for minimalist builds who prefer a clean setup.
