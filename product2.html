Product Description


Corsair Harpoon is a gaming mouse for budget-conscious gamers. Small form factor will make mouse flicking easier, a perfect trait for FPS mouse. Rubberized grip areas will ensure a firm and precise control over your mouse. There are 2 extra buttons on the right side. It's located near your thumb for fast and easy access, without disrupting your grip position. You can bind these extra buttons for certain actions inside your game, like reloading, or checking map.
